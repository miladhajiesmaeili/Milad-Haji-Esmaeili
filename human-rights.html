Human Rights

Human rights are the basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person in the world, from birth until death. They are based on shared values such as dignity, fairness, equality, and respect.

Key human rights principles include:

  • Right to life
  • Freedom of expression
  • Equality before the law
  • Protection from discrimination
