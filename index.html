About Dr. Milad Haji Esmaeili

Dr. Milad Haji Esmaeili is a prominent researcher and university professor specializing in international law, with a particular focus on oil and gas law. He is actively involved in academic research, publishing scholarly articles in reputable journals. His contributions to the field of international law are recognized and respected by his peers.

Beyond his academic pursuits, Dr. Esmaeili also engages in public outreach, sharing his knowledge and insights through online platforms like YouTube. He is passionate about educating the public on legal matters, particularly in the areas of international law and human rights.
